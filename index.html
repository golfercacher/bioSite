About Billie

Billie was born in North Dakota and moved with her parents and 7 other siblings to Elkhorn, Nebraska when she was a young child. Billie went to grade school, middle school, and high school in Elkhorn. After high school she went to Creighton University. At Creighton she majored in Chemistry. Around the time she went to Creighton she moved with her parents to Papillion, Nebraska. Billie met her future husband Sean in 1996. Billie and Sean dated for several years and they married in 2001.

Also in 2001 Billie and Sean moved into their first home in Bellevue, Nebraska. During that time they had several pets. This included two dogs named Bhoutros and Reagan. Both were Great Danes. They also had three cats named Duke, Squeaks, and Cloud. Currently Cloud is the only animal in the house. Speaking of houses, Billie and Sean built a new home in late November of 2017 in Gretna, Nebraska.

Billie’s hobbies include Yoga, running, and wine. Billie enjoys doing Yoga at One Tree Yoga. Billie has run several ultra marathons, marathons, and half marathons. iIn addition to those distances, Billie has also run numerous 10k’s, and 5k’s. Billie has visited wine country several times and if given a choice between Napa and Sonoma, would choose Sonoma. That being said some of her favorite wineries are in Napa. Some of her favorite Wineries include Matthiasson, Kokomo, St. Francis, and Ferrari Carano.

Currently Billie is into the TV show “Schitts Creek”. The show is on Pop. The last season of the show is quickly approaching and she is looking forward to the upcoming episodes. In addition to that show Billie enjoys Veronica Mars and watching Good Mythical Morning on YouTube.
